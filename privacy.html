Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Tropic Stock Bot For Grow A Garden (TSBFGAG) (“Bot,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Bot on Discord.

1. Information We Collect

We collect Discord IDs, server/channel/message IDs, user-provided settings, and public game data. No IP addresses, device info, or sensitive data are collected.

2. How We Use Your Info

We use your data to provide alerts, notifications, and settings management. Data is stored locally in SQLite and not used for ads.

3. Sharing Your Data

We don’t sell your info. We may share it with APIs, Discord, or if required by law.

4. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data but cannot guarantee 100% security.

5. Your Choices

You can manage alerts or request deletion of data via Bot commands. Removing the Bot revokes consent.

6. Children

The Bot is not for users under 13. If we find children’s data, we will delete it.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use after updates means acceptance.

8. Contact

For questions, reach out at: discord.gg/growabiggarden
